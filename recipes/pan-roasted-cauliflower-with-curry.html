Pan Roasted Cauliflower with Curry

Description

This roasted cauliflower with curry is simple and melts in your mouth. It is a perfect as a spicy vegan side dish or even as a quick and easy main course.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon yellow curry powder/li>
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Â½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 pound cauliflower florets, cut into 1/2-inch slices

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil.
  2. Combine olive oil, curry powder, lemon juice, salt, and pepper in a bowl. Add cauliflower and toss until well coated. Transfer to the prepared baking sheet and spread in a single layer.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until golden and crispy, about 25 minutes.
